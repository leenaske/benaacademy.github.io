Our Impact projects

Check out some of our awesome projects that we also do.

  • Campain Against FGM

    Nyabosongo Bena Academy is committed to the protection and empowerment of young girls. Through educational campaigns and community engagement, we raise awareness and work to end the practice of Female Genital Mutilation (FGM), advocating for the rights and health of every girl.

  • Campain Against Gender Based Violence

    We believe in building a safe and supportive community. Our Gender-Based Violence Awareness programs educate students and families about the importance of respect, equality, and safety. Through workshops and counseling, we strive to create a culture of zero tolerance for violence.

    • >

  • support to vulnerable student

    Every child deserves access to quality education. We actively support vulnerable students, providing scholarships and resources to ensure that financial challenges do not hinder their potential. Through this initiative, we aim to make education accessible and transformative for all.
